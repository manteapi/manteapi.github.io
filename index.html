Decomposition of force computation

Notations

  • $f$ stands for forces
  • $q$ stands for positions
  • $E$ stands for elastic energy
  • $F$ stands for deformation gradient
  • $\epsilon$ stands for strain

Decomposition

We can write : $$ f = -\frac{dE}{dq} = - \left(\frac{dF}{dq}\right)^{T} \left(\frac{d\epsilon}{dF}\right)^{T} \left(\frac{dE}{d\epsilon}\right)$$ And I think that : $$ \sigma = \frac{dE}{d\epsilon} $$
